Care Sector Staffing in Ireland

Ireland's care and social services sector faces one of the most severe staffing crises in the country. Residential care homes, home care agencies, and disability services are operating with chronic vacancies that cannot be filled from the domestic labour market alone.

Filipino care workers bring exactly what this sector needs: genuine compassion, patience, and a caring culture that runs deep. Many come with direct experience in care settings and hold qualifications in healthcare or social services. CA Recruitment places vetted Filipino care workers across the spectrum of care and support roles, managing the full permit process so you can focus on delivering services.

Why Filipino Care Workers?

Natural Compassion

Filipino culture places enormous value on caring for others, particularly the elderly and vulnerable. This shows up every day in care settings — it's not something you can train into someone.

English Proficiency

Clear communication with residents, clients, and colleagues is critical in care settings. Filipino care workers are English fluent as standard.

Care Qualifications

Many Filipino candidates hold formal care or health-related qualifications and bring direct residential or community care experience from the Philippines or previous overseas placements.

Commitment & Stability

Permit-route workers are motivated to stay and build a career. The turnover and reliability problems common with agency care staff are much less of an issue.
