Why Filipino Finance Professionals?

Strong Qualifications

The Philippines has a well-developed accountancy education system. Many Filipino finance professionals hold CPA qualifications and degrees from accredited universities.

English Proficiency

All financial reporting, communication, and system work is in English. Filipino finance professionals are entirely comfortable with English as their working language.

Multinational Experience

Many Filipino accountants have worked for international companies and BPO operations, bringing exposure to global financial standards and multi-currency environments.

Detail-Oriented

Accuracy and attention to detail are fundamental in finance roles. Filipino finance professionals are known for their methodical approach and commitment to getting numbers right.

Qualified accountants and finance professionals typically qualify for the Critical Skills Employment Permit (CSEP), which requires a minimum salary of €40,904 per annum and does not require a Labour Market Needs Test.
