The role must meet the minimum salary threshold (€36,605 per annum), be on the DETE eligible occupations list, and pass the 28-day Labour Market Needs Test. We'll confirm eligibility for your specific role during your free consultation.

General operatives, formwork carpenters, concreters, groundworkers, steel fixers, and site management roles are among those placed via the GEP route. Some specialist or higher-paid roles may qualify for the Critical Skills Employment Permit. Contact us to verify your specific role.
