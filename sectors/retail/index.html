Retail Staffing Challenges in Ireland

Irish retail has faced persistent recruitment difficulties across all store formats — from independent shops to large supermarket chains. High turnover, part-time churn, and difficulty filling consistent full-time roles have left many operators short-staffed at floor level and in supervisory positions.

Overseas workers bring a strong work ethic, genuine customer service orientation, and excellent English — all qualities that translate directly into a retail environment. Many have prior retail and customer service experience internationally, and they tend to stay in roles once placed through the employment permit route.

Why Filipino Retail Workers?

Customer Service Orientation

Filipino culture places great emphasis on courtesy and service. This carries directly into retail — customer interactions, complaint handling, and general floor presence are natural strengths.

English Proficiency

English is an official language in the Philippines and is used in schools, business, and media. Customer communication and team coordination are never a barrier.

Reliability

Workers who come through the employment permit route have invested significantly in the opportunity. Consistent attendance and commitment to the role are the norm, not the exception.

Stable Workforce

Unlike locally sourced seasonal or part-time retail workers, permit holders tend to stay. Lower turnover means less disruption and reduced ongoing recruitment cost for your store.

Only specialist and management-level retail roles qualify for the General Employment Permit (GEP). Basic roles such as retail assistants, cashiers, stock room operatives, and customer service representatives are on DETE's Ineligible Occupations List and cannot be sponsored for a permit. We'll confirm eligibility for your specific role during the free consultation.

The eligible retail roles under the GEP are: Senior Retail Manager (e.g. Area Manager), specialist technical sales roles requiring specific expertise, Retail Pharmacists, Butchers/Boners in specialist food retail environments, and Bakery Managers/Specialists. Standard floor staff, cashiers, and stock room roles are not permit-eligible. Contact us to confirm whether your vacancy falls within the eligible categories.
