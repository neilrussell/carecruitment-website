Hospitality Labour Shortage in Ireland

Ireland's hospitality sector has been operating with significant understaffing since the reopening post-pandemic. Hotels, restaurants, and food service operations across the country are routinely short-staffed, particularly in kitchen and housekeeping roles that are hard to fill locally.

Filipino workers have a long and strong tradition in hospitality — from five-star hotels to cruise ships. Their service orientation, English fluency, and professional approach make them a natural fit for Irish hospitality operations.
