Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data

Last updated: 28 April 2026

CA Recruitment (trading name of Madscope Marketing Ltd, registered in Ireland) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018.

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under Irish and EU data protection law.

1. Who We Are

Data Controller: Madscope Marketing Ltd t/a CA Recruitment
Address: 14 The Meadows, Ballyporeen, Co. Tipperary, E21 E260, Ireland
Phone: +353 89 416 6124
Email: info@carecruitment.com

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Contact Form

When you complete our contact form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Your message / enquiry details

2.2 Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how visitors use our website. This collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website or search query
  • Browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Anonymised IP address

This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. Analytics cookies are only set if you have accepted cookies via our cookie consent banner.

2.3 Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Cookie consent preference — a localStorage entry (ca_cookie_consent) that records whether you accepted or declined analytics cookies. This is essential to honour your choice and is not shared with third parties.
  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*) — set only with your consent. Used to distinguish visitors and measure site usage. Data is processed by Google LLC under their privacy policy.
  • Google Tag Manager — a tag management container that loads analytics scripts. No cookies are set by GTM itself.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

DataPurposeLegal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)
Contact form detailsRespond to your enquiry and provide our recruitment servicesLegitimate interests / pre-contractual steps
Analytics dataUnderstand site usage and improve the user experienceConsent
Cookie preferenceHonour your cookie consent choiceLegal obligation / legitimate interests

4. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Contact form enquiries — retained for up to 2 years from the date of enquiry, or until you ask us to delete your data.
  • Analytics data — retained by Google Analytics for 14 months (our default setting), then automatically deleted.
  • Cookie preference — stored in your browser's localStorage until you clear it or it is updated.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Netlify, Inc. — our website hosting provider, which processes form submissions. Netlify stores form data on servers within their infrastructure. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Google LLC — Google Analytics processes analytics data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Irish authorities — if required by law (e.g., a court order or DETE inquiry).

No data is transferred to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. International Transfers

Google Analytics data may be processed in the United States. Google LLC complies with the EU–US Data Privacy Framework. Netlify processes data under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw at any time (e.g., clear your ca_cookie_consent localStorage entry to reset your cookie preference)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@carecruitment.com. We will respond within one month.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (DPC) — Ireland's supervisory authority — at www.dataprotection.ie.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Our website is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are transmitted securely to Netlify's infrastructure.

9. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., DETE, WRC, government resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and recommend you review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Significant changes will be communicated via a notice on our website.
