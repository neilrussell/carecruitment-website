Why generalist overseas agencies miss the care worker tier

If you run a residential care home, home care service, or disability support organisation, you may have contacted other overseas recruitment agencies and hit a wall. Here's why that happens — and why it won't happen with us.

Most overseas agencies focus on construction, engineering, and agriculture. These sectors typically involve roles at or above the standard General Employment Permit (GEP) salary threshold of €36,605. Care workers operate on different terms. The care assistant and home carer roles qualify for a reduced GEP threshold of €16.12 per hour — €32,691 per annum on a 39-hour week, or €33,529.60 on a 40-hour week — but only if the employer and role structure meet the specific DETE requirements for this tier.

Agencies that don't know this tier don't work it. They either quote you standard GEP costs that don't apply, or they simply tell you care assistant roles don't qualify — which is incorrect.

CA Recruitment specifically serves the care worker tier. We know the permit route, we know the salary thresholds, and we know the HIQA and DETE compliance requirements that affect care placements. If you're a care home operator or home care provider who has been turned away or given incorrect information by other agencies, talk to us.

How the hiring process works

  • 1

    Free Consultation

    We assess your role, your sector, and the appropriate permit route. We'll tell you what's involved, what it costs, and what a realistic timeline looks like. No obligation, no charge.

  • 2

    Labour Market Needs Test

    Before a GEP application can be submitted, the role must be advertised for 28 days on Jobs Ireland and at least one other platform. We manage this requirement in full — you don't post anything yourself.

  • 3

    Candidate Sourcing & Vetting

    We source candidates from the Philippines through our community networks, interview them, verify their qualifications and references, and present only those who match your role. We have people on the ground in the Philippines — this is not a database search.

  • 4

    DETE Permit Application

    We prepare and submit the employment permit application through the DETE online system. We manage all supporting documentation, follow up on the application, and handle any queries from DETE directly.

  • 5

    Pre-Departure & Arrival Support

    We support the worker through visa processing, pre-departure preparation, and arrival in Ireland. We stay in contact through the first 90 days to make sure the placement is working for both sides.
