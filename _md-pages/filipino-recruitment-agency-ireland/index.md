## Employment Permit Facts (2026)

Most placements use the General Employment Permit (GEP). Key facts:

- **Minimum salary (standard GEP):** €36,605 per year
- **Minimum salary (care assistants, home carers, horticulture):** €16.12/hr — €32,691/yr on a 39-hour week, €33,529.60 on a 40-hour week
- **Labour Market Needs Test:** 28 consecutive days advertising required before application submission
- **DETE processing time:** 8 to 12 weeks (check live dates at enterprise.gov.ie)
- **End-to-end timeline:** 6 to 8 months (care/hospitality); 6 to 8 months (agriculture/construction)
- **Government permit fee:** €1,000 paid by employer directly to DETE
- **50/50 rule:** at least 50% of the employer's workforce must be EEA nationals at time of application
