## Work Permit Facts

The General Employment Permit (GEP) is the standard route for Filipino workers in Ireland.

- Minimum annual salary: €36,605 for most roles; €16.12/hr (€32,691/yr on a 39-hour week) for care assistants, home carers, horticulture workers, and certain other categories
- Labour Market Needs Test: 28-day advertising requirement before application
- 50/50 rule: at least 50% of the employer's total workforce must be EEA nationals at time of application
- DETE application fee: €1,000, paid by the employer at submission
- Processing time: approximately 10 to 12 weeks for new GEP applications (check current dates at enterprise.gov.ie)
- D-visa: applied for by the worker at the Irish Embassy in Manila after GEP approval; typically 2 to 4 weeks

Full permit process details: [Work Permit Guide for Irish Employers](https://carecruitment.com/work-permit-guide/)
