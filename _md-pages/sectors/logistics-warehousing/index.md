# Filipino Logistics and Warehousing Workers for Ireland — CA Recruitment

CA Recruitment places vetted Filipino workers in logistics, warehousing, and distribution roles with Irish employers. Permit type: General Employment Permit.

## Roles Filled

- Warehouse Operatives
- Forklift Drivers
- Delivery Drivers
- Logistics Coordinators
- Stock Controllers
- Distribution Workers

## Permit and Salary Information

- **Permit type:** General Employment Permit (GEP)
- **Minimum annual salary:** €36,605
- **Labour Market Needs Test:** required — 28-day advertising on Jobs Ireland + one additional platform
- **50/50 rule:** at least 50% of the business workforce must be EEA nationals at time of application
- **Government permit fee:** €1,000 paid by employer to DETE
- **Timeline:** 6 to 8 months from initial consultation to worker starting

## Why This Works

Logistics and warehousing in Ireland faces persistent labour shortages driven by e-commerce growth and distribution network expansion. Filipino workers bring strong work ethic, English proficiency, and adaptability to shift-based and physically demanding work environments.

## Key Facts

- English is an official language of the Philippines — no language barrier
- All candidates have qualifications and references verified before shortlisting
- CA Recruitment manages the full DETE permit process — the employer's involvement is the consultation, shortlist review, and interviews
