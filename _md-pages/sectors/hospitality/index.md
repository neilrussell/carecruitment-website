# Filipino Chefs and Hospitality Workers for Ireland — CA Recruitment

CA Recruitment places vetted Filipino chefs with Irish hotels, restaurants, and hospitality businesses. Permit type: General Employment Permit. Timeline: 6 to 8 months.

## Chef Grades Eligible for an Employment Permit

DETE allows the following chef grades under the General Employment Permit:

| Grade | Minimum experience required |
|---|---|
| Executive Chef | 5 years relevant experience (outside fast-food) |
| Head Chef | 5 years relevant experience (outside fast-food) |
| Sous Chef | 5 years relevant experience (outside fast-food) |
| Chef de Partie | 2 years relevant experience |
| Commis Chef | 2 years relevant experience |

**Important:** Bar staff, waiters and waitresses, kitchen porters, housekeepers, hotel receptionists, and catering assistants are all on DETE's Ineligible Occupations List. They cannot be sponsored for an employment permit. Only chef grades qualify.

## Permit and Salary Information

- **Permit type:** General Employment Permit
- **Minimum annual salary:** €36,605 (standard GEP rate for chef roles)
- **Labour Market Needs Test:** required — 28-day advertising on Jobs Ireland + one additional platform
- **50/50 rule:** yes — at least 50% of the business workforce must be EEA nationals at time of application
- **Government permit fee:** €1,000 paid by employer to DETE

## Timeline

From initial consultation to the chef starting: **6 to 8 months**. This includes the 28-day LMNT, 8 to 12 weeks of DETE processing, visa approval, and travel from the Philippines.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do Filipino chefs have real hospitality experience?**
Yes. To qualify for an employment permit, chefs must meet DETE minimum experience requirements: 5 years for Executive Chef, Head Chef, and Sous Chef grades; 2 years for Chef de Partie and Commis Chef. These must be outside fast-food operations. CA Recruitment verifies experience and references before anyone reaches a shortlist.

**What chef grades qualify for an employment permit in Ireland?**
Executive Chef, Head Chef, Sous Chef (minimum 5 years relevant experience); Chef de Partie, Commis Chef (minimum 2 years relevant experience). Experience must be outside fast-food operations.

**Can I hire bar staff, waiters, or kitchen porters from overseas on an employment permit?**
No. Bar staff, waiters and waitresses, kitchen porters, housekeepers, hotel receptionists, and catering assistants are all on DETE's Ineligible Occupations List.

**Will overseas chefs fit into my kitchen team?**
Filipino chefs placed through CA Recruitment are sourced against the specific kitchen environment — cuisine style, brigade size, and pace of service. Many have worked in international hotel groups and fine dining environments.

**How long does it take to hire an overseas chef in Ireland?**
From initial consultation to the chef starting: 6 to 8 months. This includes the 28-day LMNT, DETE processing, visa approval, and travel from the Philippines.

**What if the chef doesn't work out?**

**How much does it cost to hire through CA Recruitment?**
