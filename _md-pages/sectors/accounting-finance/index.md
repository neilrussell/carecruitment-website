# Filipino Accounting and Finance Workers for Ireland — CA Recruitment

CA Recruitment places vetted Filipino accounting and finance professionals with Irish employers. Permit type: General Employment Permit or Critical Skills Employment Permit depending on the role. No fee until placement.

## Roles Filled

- Accounts Assistants
- Bookkeepers
- Payroll Administrators
- Financial Controllers
- Management Accountants
- Credit Controllers

## Permit and Salary Information

- **Permit type:** General Employment Permit (GEP) for most roles; Critical Skills Employment Permit (CSEP) for senior qualified roles on the CSEP list
- **Minimum annual salary (GEP):** €36,605
- **Minimum annual salary (CSEP — listed occupation):** €40,904
- **Labour Market Needs Test:** required for GEP; not required for CSEP
- **50/50 rule:** applies to both permit types
- **Government permit fee:** €1,000 (GEP); amount varies for CSEP
- **Timeline:** 6 to 8 months (GEP); 2 to 4 months (CSEP)

## Why Filipino Accounting Professionals

The Philippines has a large pool of accounting and finance professionals educated to international standards. Many hold CPA (Certified Public Accountant) qualifications under the Philippine CPA framework. English is the medium of instruction throughout Philippine tertiary education.
