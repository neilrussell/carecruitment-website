The process

Straightforward from start to first shift.

No guesswork, no mountains of admin on your side. We handle every step, from candidate sourcing to work permit compliance.

01

Tell us what you need

Free consultation — you tell us the role, the hours, the skills required, and the start date. We advise on permit type, timeline, and realistic expectations upfront.

02

We find & screen candidates

Our team on the ground in the Philippines sources, interviews, and vets candidates in person. You only see a shortlist of people we'd put our name to.

03

You interview. We handle the rest.

You pick your candidate. We manage every permit application, document submission, DETE liaison, and visa step. You get updates — not admin.

04

Your worker arrives, ready to go

We provide onboarding guidance and check-ins during the first 90 days. If anything goes wrong in that period, we replace your worker free of charge.
