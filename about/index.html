The agency that knows both sides of this equation.

Most recruitment agencies that claim to specialise in overseas workers have never set foot in the Philippines. They work from databases, not relationships. They know the paperwork, not the people.

CA Recruitment was built differently. We are Filipino-owned and Ireland-based — which means we have genuine connections in the communities where we source candidates, and genuine understanding of what Irish employers are dealing with when they take on a non-EU worker for the first time.

We handle everything: sourcing, vetting, employment permit applications, DETE liaison, employment law compliance, pre-departure support, and post-placement check-ins. You focus on your business. We handle the rest.

Our candidates are not pulled from a CV database. They are interviewed, reference-checked, and in many cases assessed in person on the ground in the Philippines before they are ever presented to you. We only put forward people we'd hire ourselves.

Compliance isn't a box we tick. It's built into every placement.

Irish employers taking on non-EU workers for the first time often don't know what they don't know. The employment permit system, WRC obligations, salary thresholds, and DETE requirements all carry risk if handled incorrectly.

We manage compliance end to end — and we keep your business protected at every stage of the process, from application to onboarding to the first 90 days of employment.

DETE Permit Process Managed

Every placement is structured in line with Irish employment permit law and DETE requirements for non-EU workers.

DETE-Compliant Process

We manage all employment permit applications through the DETE (Department of Enterprise, Trade and Employment) employment permit online system.

No Fee Until Placement

You incur no cost until your worker is successfully placed and starts their first day. Our interests are fully aligned with yours.

90-Day Replacement Guarantee

If a worker placed by us leaves or doesn't work out within the first 90 days, we find a replacement at no additional charge.
